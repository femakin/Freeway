Welcome to Freeway Books!


Freeway Books has expressed dissapointment at the cancellation of all stores in the country by the federal government due to Covid-19 More Info on Covid-19. generally known as coronavirus but accepted that it was the "right Call" as the government of Nigeria works on containment of the coronavirus Outbreak.In Compliance with the directive of our federal government, Femi Books is temporarily suspending all activities accross the globe.

Regards this, we direct all our staff to stay off the company and start working from their respective homes. All Staff are to note that the following mechanism has been put in place regards this new decision.

  • All Staff are to check the announcement on this site for necessary updates.
  • Managers to log in to the company portal with their provided password for the approved projects and waiting projects.
