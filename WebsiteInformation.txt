FreeWay Books

A book Publishing adn editing Company designed with HTML, CSS and JavaScript with
the intention of disseminating information to staff of the company as they work
remotely in their various countries.
The features on this site include but not limited to 
a Nav bar consisting of Homepage, General Information, Managers Portal, About us and 
Contacts.
Clicking on General Infor. takes the junior staff and every other persons 
to the announcement section where they get to known the necessary updates 
of the company.
Manager's Portal are strictly for senior staff of the company, they login
into the portal with their email and password to have access to their 
official information from their various countries so as to work remotely.
About Us; This is the contact section of the company.
